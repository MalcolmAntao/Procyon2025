ABOUT US

  • Welcome to Procyon 2K25, a celebration of creativity, talent, and cultural diversity! Our annual cultural extravaganza brings together students, performers, and enthusiasts to showcase their skills in music, dance, art, drama, and more.
  • This year’s theme, Carnival, promises to captivate you with electrifying performances, and unforgettable memories. Whether you’re here to perform, compete, or simply experience the magic, there’s something for everyone!
  • So join us as we celebrate the vibrant spirit of our community and make Procyon 2K25 a festival to remember!
