About Us

  • Welcome to Procyon 2K25, a celebration of creativity, talent, and cultural diversity! Our annual cultural extravaganza brings together students, performers, and enthusiasts to showcase their skills in music, dance, art, drama, and more.

  • This year’s theme, Carnival, promises to captivate you with electrifying performances, and unforgettable memories. Whether you’re here to perform, compete, or simply experience the magic, there’s something for everyone!

  • So join us as we celebrate the vibrant spirit of our community and make Procyon 2K25 a festival to remember!

    • Procyon Cultural Fiesta

    Procyon is an inter-department cultural fiesta, wherein students participate in 17 different events to showcase their talents in various cultural activities on a common platform. This year, several activities are being organized, including:

    • Ghumat Aarti
    • The Voice
    • Skit
    • Battle of Bands
    • Food Wizard
    • Hoist the Banner
    • The Art of Colors
    • Mad-Lipz
    • Lights Camera Action
    • Slow Mo
    • Step Up Revolution
    • Mr. and Ms. Procyon
    • Rock the Ramp
    • Street Play
    • Treasure Hunt
    • Face Painting
    • Framed (On-Campus)

    This event is organized by the students, for the students, providing a platform for creativity, collaboration, and celebration of diverse talents.
